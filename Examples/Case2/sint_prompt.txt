Step 1: Initialization Phase.
Each Consultant formulates their main position (max 2 sentences) on Objective within their Focus. Generate minimum 3 ideas per agent with Probability ratings (0-1) and reasoning. All formulations, ideas, and replies strictly in english with style adaptations per agent Style.

Step 2: Divergence and Conflict Assessment Phase.
Each Consultant assigns Complementarity Rating (1-10) to all opponent positions and evaluates probability distributions.
Conflict Assessment: If at least one agent assigned Complementarity Rating less than 3 (Defective/Dangerous) to opponents' positions, transition to Step 3A (Criticism Phase). Otherwise, transition to Step 3B (Integration Phase - Default Path).

Step 3A: Criticism Phase (Conflict Scenario).
All consultants provide collective criticism. Each Consultant: 1) Highlights the best thesis from opponents (rate 7-10/10). 2) Points out the most vulnerable thesis (rate 1-3/10). 3) Proposes compromise (max 2 sentences) integrating elements across perspectives. All theses must comply with PCG and reference Context facts. Generate vivid rhetorical replies per agent Style (minimum 3 replies per agent per round).

Step 3B: Integration Phase (Consensus Scenario).
Consultants do not criticize. Each Consultant integrates relevant theses (Rating greater than or equal to 3), prioritizing by importance to Objective. All theses must comply with PCG.

Step 4: Iterative Convergence/Final Synthesis.
Conduct maximum 5 debate rounds (from Dynamics iterations_limit). Before each round, LLM must generate brief Summary of Progress for context preservation. Each round: Generate narrative agent replies (minimum 3 per agent, with vivid rhetoric per Style). Example A1: "History screams warnings we refuse to hear - Libya's collapse proves intervention breeds chaos, not salvation. Prove me wrong with evidence, not moral platitudes!" Example A2: "Every moment of non-intervention in genocide makes us complicit - sovereignty ends where mass graves begin!" Example A3: "Who intervenes reveals more than why - Western intervention selectivity exposes neocolonial patterns grounded in fact_4." All agent replies in english with appropriate rhetorical style. Track rating evolution across rounds. Upon failure (Consensus less than 7/10 after 5 rounds) form fallback partial_consensus with documented minority opinion. Since verbose='true', output full rounds in DebateLog for visibility with engaging rhetorical exchanges.

Extract only PCG-valid compromise theses with rating greater than or equal to 7/10.
