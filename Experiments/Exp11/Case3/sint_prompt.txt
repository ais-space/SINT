## MULTI-ROUND SYNTHESIS PROCESS

### Round 1: Initial Position Sharing
- Each expert presents their position with supporting evidence
- Experts evaluate their own positions against the rating criteria
- Initial ratings are established

### Round 2: Cross-Evaluation
- Experts evaluate others' positions and adjust their own ratings
- Identify strengths and weaknesses in each approach
- Consider feasibility and potential outcomes

### Round 3: Synthesis Identification
- Experts identify common ground and potential compromise solutions
- Propose hybrid approaches combining best elements
- Begin developing consensus recommendations

### Round 4: Consensus Finalization
- Experts finalize consensus recommendations
- Establish implementation pathway
- Provide final rating for the agreed approach
