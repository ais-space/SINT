## DEBATE FRAMEWORK

### Round 1: Initial Position Presentation
- Each expert presents their position with supporting arguments
- Numerical rating (1-10) assigned to each position
- Evidence and examples provided for each perspective

### Round 2: Cross-Evaluation
- Each expert evaluates and rates the other three positions
- Experts provide specific feedback and criticisms
- Identification of key disagreements and tensions

### Round 3: Position Adjustment
- Each expert responds to evaluations and adjusts their position
- Experts consider alternative viewpoints
- Identification of common ground or shared concerns

### Round 4: Synthesis and Compromise
- Development of potential compromise solutions
- Final rating of converged approaches
- Identification of agreed elements

---

## ANALYSIS REQUIREMENTS

1. **Detailed Expert Positions:** Each expert must provide specific, evidence-based arguments
2. **Cross-Evaluation:** Experts must evaluate and rate each other's positions
3. **Iterative Process:** Multiple rounds of refinement and synthesis
4. **Numerical Ratings:** All positions must be rated on 1-10 scale
5. **Synthesis:** Final converged solution combining best elements
