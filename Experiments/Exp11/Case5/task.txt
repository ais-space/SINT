# Task: Artificial Intelligence Regulation: Four-Stakeholder Analysis with Conflicting Interests

## Description
Regulation of AI technologies from four distinct stakeholder perspectives with numerical ratings and cross-evaluation.

## Experts
### Expert 1: Technology Entrepreneur
**Position:** Light-touch regulation to preserve innovation
**Arguments:**
1. Over-regulation stifles technological advancement
2. Global competitiveness requires agility
3. Self-regulation by industry is more effective

**Rating:** 6.5/10

### Expert 2: Civil Rights Advocate
**Position:** Robust regulation to protect individual rights
**Arguments:**
1. AI poses risks to privacy and civil liberties
2. Algorithmic bias requires legal remedies
3. Individual rights must be protected

**Rating:** 8.5/10

### Expert 3: Government Policy Maker
**Position:** Principles-based regulation with adaptive frameworks
**Arguments:**
1. Regulation must be adaptable to new developments
2. International coordination is necessary
3. Balance between innovation and protection

**Rating:** 7.5/10

### Expert 4: Academic Researcher
**Position:** Evidence-based regulation with transparency
**Arguments:**
1. Regulation should be based on empirical evidence
2. Transparency in AI systems is essential
3. Interdisciplinary approach to assessment

**Rating:** 8.0/10
