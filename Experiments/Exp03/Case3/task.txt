Title: University Research Funding Allocation Posture
Objective: Integrate excellence, equity, and strategic alignment into a consensus model for allocating internal research funds.
Context: Peer review bias, early-career support, core facilities, societal impact, matching grants.
Methodology: Debates (N=3) with Step 3B (Consensus); integration and prioritization.
Experts: Research Policy Director; Faculty Representative (STEM/Humanities balance); Finance and Grants Officer.
Constraints: PCG; ratings and cross-evaluation at Step 2; Dual Output + Verification.
Expected Outcomes: Prioritized funding rules with measurable targets and review cadence.
Language: English only.
