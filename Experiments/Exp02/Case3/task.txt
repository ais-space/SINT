Title: Post-Pandemic Rapid Transit Redesign
Objective: Integrate ridership recovery, cost control, and reliability into a consensus roadmap for a metropolitan rapid transit network.
Context: Peak/off-peak patterns, remote work shifts, maintenance backlogs, fare policies, accessibility, and equity goals.
Methodology: Debates (N=3) with Step 3B (Consensus); no criticism; emphasize integration and prioritization.
Experts: Transit Operations Director; Urban Mobility Planner; Public Finance Analyst.
Constraints: PCG required; ratings (1-10) and cross-evaluation at Step 2; Dual Output with Verification Report.
Expected Outcomes: Prioritized investment and service plan (headways, fleet, maintenance windows) with performance targets.
Language: English only.
