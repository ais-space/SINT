Title: Water Allocation Strategy under Multi-Year Drought
Objective: Conduct a conflict-oriented debate to derive legally robust and socially acceptable allocation strategies for river-basin water during prolonged drought.
Context: Senior/junior water rights, ecological flow requirements, agricultural vs. urban demand, emergency reserves, enforcement capacity.
Methodology: Debates (N=4) with Step 3A (Conflict), at least 2 rounds with divergence ratings and compromise synthesis.
Experts: Water Rights Legal Scholar; Agricultural Hydrologist; Municipal Infrastructure Planner; Environmental Conservation Scientist.
Constraints: PCG required; detailed ratings and cross-evaluation; Dual Output with Verification Report.
Expected Outcomes: Ranked portfolio of allocation rules, enforcement mechanisms, and drought stage triggers with KPIs.
Language: English only.
