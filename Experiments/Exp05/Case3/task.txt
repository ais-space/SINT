Title: University Micro-credential Framework
Objective: Integrate academic quality, employability, and accessibility into a consensus framework for micro-credentials.
Context: Stackability, accreditation, employer partnerships, tuition models, student support.
Methodology: Debates (N=3) with Step 3B (Consensus); integrate and prioritize.
Experts: Academic Quality Lead; Career Services and Employer Relations; Student Success and Accessibility Officer.
Constraints: PCG; ratings and cross-evaluation at Step 2; Dual Output with Verification.
Expected Outcomes: Policy set with measurable targets and review cadence.
Language: English only.
