Title: Hospital Triage Redesign for Surge Events
Objective: Integrate timeliness, equity, and survival outcomes into a consensus triage redesign for hospital surge conditions.
Context: ED bottlenecks, ICU capacity, staff shortages, ethical frameworks, data integration.
Methodology: Debates (N=3) with Step 3B (Consensus); integrate and prioritize without criticism.
Experts: Emergency Medicine Lead; Health Systems Operations Analyst; Bioethics and Equity Officer.
Constraints: PCG; ratings and cross-evaluation at Step 2; Dual Output + Verification.
Expected Outcomes: Prioritized triage protocol changes with measurable targets and monitoring.
Language: English only.
